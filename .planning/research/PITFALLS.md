# Pitfalls Research: Temple Management

## Common Mistakes
- **Over-complicating Payments**: Integrating complex payment gateways with high fees and compliance hurdles.
  *Prevention*: Stick to direct UPI and manual reconciliation for now.
- **Ignoring User Tech-Savviness**: Building complex, data-heavy dashboards for staff who are used to simple paper ledgers.
  *Prevention*: Retain the simple glassmorphism UI, leverage voice dictation, and keep localization (Kannada) front and center.
- **Security Misconfigurations**: Exposing an on-premise server to the internet via direct port forwarding, leading to attacks.
  *Prevention*: Use secure, authenticated reverse tunnels (e.g., Cloudflare Tunnels).
