# Features Research: Temple Management

## Table Stakes
- **Devotee Registry**: Managing personal details, gotras, and contact info.
- **Seva Management**: Booking services, managing schedules, and tracking fulfilment.
- **Financials**: Receipt generation, tracking donations, cash/cheque management.

## Differentiators
- **Multi-lingual UI**: Regional language support (e.g., Kannada) is crucial for lay users.
- **Voice Input**: Speech-to-text for rapid data entry by non-tech-savvy staff.
- **Direct UPI Integration**: Avoiding complex payment gateways in favor of direct peer-to-peer or peer-to-merchant UPI tracking.

## Anti-Features
- **Enterprise ERP Integration**: Over-complicating the system with SAP/Oracle integrations is unnecessary and adds administrative burden.
