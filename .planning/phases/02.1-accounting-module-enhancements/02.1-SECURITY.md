| T-2.1.1 | Broken Access Control | `/api/accounting/close-day` | MITIGATE | Add strict role checking `Depends(auth.get_current_user)` enforcing `user.role == 'admin'` to prevent staff from calling close-day. | CLOSED |

| T-2.1.3 | SQL Injection | CSV Streaming Export | MITIGATE | Use SQLAlchemy's built-in ORM parameterization exclusively and escape special CSV characters to prevent Excel/CSV formula injections. | CLOSED |
